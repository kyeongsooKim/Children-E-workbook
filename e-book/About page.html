Student E-Workbook

How to use this E-workbook

 
This E-Workbook is designed to to reinforce PTP(Prevention Through Puppetry) lessons and provide additonal activities for children.
The content in the E-workbook is based on the actual textbook, but it provides the perfect way to experience it through electric devices.
Students can learn the lessons and solve the quizzes by doing several different activities, such as drawing, writing, and drag-and-drop.
We hope that you enjoy the activities and hopefully together we can promote youth character education.
If you have any questions or comments feel free to contact us. Thank you.
