LESSON

Understanding and expressing emotions
         Directions:
   1.    Review the Feelings Garden chart in the workbook. These are
emotions that we all have from time to time, but are discussed
a little less.
   2.    Go over each feeling individually and ask questions about
feelings.
 
 
 
 
 
 
Suggested Review:
Point to individual feeling faces and ask the following questions:

  1. What Feeling is this?
  2. How can you tell they are feeling that way?
  3. What happens when you feel this way?
  4. Is it ok to feel this way?
  5. What are the healthy things you do when you get these feelings?
