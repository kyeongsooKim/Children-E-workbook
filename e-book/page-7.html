LESSON:

Conflict resolution

Activity: Sunshine steps
Directions:
Answer the questions to the best of your ability and
review the with the class.
 
1.  What is a friend ?
2.  Can you have more than one friend ?
3.  Do friends change ? Do you always have the same friend ?
4.  Can friends have other friends ?
5.  Do you have to do everything your friend says ?
6.  Is is okay to feel sad or mad if your friend is mean to you ?
7.  How should you treat a friend ?
8.  What do you do if your friend is mean to you ?
9.  Should you say mean things about friends to another friend ? why ?
10. Do you have to do everything your friend asks because you are their
      friend ? What if they want you to do something that may not be the
      best decision ?
11. Is it always easy to have a friend ?
